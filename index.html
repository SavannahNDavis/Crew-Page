The Straw Hat Pirates

Who We Are

The Straw Hats are pirates who don't abide by the laws of the World Government. We are a very infamous and powerful pirate crew that originated from the East Blue looking for adventure and the One Piece. Although we're considered villains by the World Government and the general public who are not familiar with us, we are kindhearted for the most part and only fight in self-defense or vengeance for our friends. Our current bounties now total up to 8,816,001,000 Berries.
